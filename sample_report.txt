
TECH CORP Q3 2024 PERFORMANCE REPORT

1. Executive Summary
Tech Corp has delivered a strong performance in Q3 2024, with revenue growing by 15% year-over-year to $4.2 million. The growth was primarily driven by the launch of our new "Nexus AI" product line.

2. Financial Highlights
- Total Revenue: $4.2M (+15% YoY)
- Operating Expenses: $2.8M (+5% YoY)
- Net Profit: $1.4M (+41% YoY)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Reduced from $150 to $120.

3. Product Performance
- Nexus AI (New): Generated $800k in its first quarter.
- Cloud Services: Steady growth of 8%.
- Legacy Software: Decline of 3% as customers migrate to cloud.

4. Strategic Risks
- Supply chain disruptions for hardware components remain a concern.
- Competitor "CyberSys" has announced a similar AI tool launching in Q4.

5. Future Outlook
We project Q4 revenue to hit $4.8M. The focus will be on aggressive marketing for Nexus AI and expanding the sales team in the European market.
