CUSTOMER FEEDBACK AND CHURN ANALYSIS - Q1 2026

1. OVERVIEW
This report details customer feedback gathered during Q1 2026. We analyzed 150 customer reviews and support interactions to identify key pain points and churn risks.

2. SEGMENT A: ENTERPRISE CUSTOMERS
Enterprise clients are generally satisfied with product performance, rating it 4.5/5. However, they expressed concerns over API rate limits. 
- Acme Corp (ID: ENT-902) noted that they hit rate limits twice during peak hours, causing a delay in their ETL pipelines. They requested a higher tier.
- Globex Corporation (ID: ENT-331) requested single sign-on (SSO) support for better access controls.

3. SEGMENT B: MID-MARKET CUSTOMERS
Mid-market clients have a higher churn risk (currently estimated at 12%). The primary complaint is the lack of dedicated account manager support.
- Initech (ID: MID-104) is unhappy with response times for critical bugs, which currently averages 18 hours. They are actively comparing alternatives.
- Hooli (ID: MID-705) mentioned they might downgrade their subscription unless we release the automated scheduling feature by March.

4. RECOMMENDATIONS
- Increase API rate limits for Enterprise customers by 50% immediately.
- Assign a dedicated support specialist to high-risk mid-market clients like Initech.
- Roll out the automated scheduling module before March 15, 2026.
